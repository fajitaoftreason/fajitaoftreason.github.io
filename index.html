About Me

I graduated from University of Central Florida with an Interdisciplinary Studies major with a minor in Computer Science, and focus areas in Psychology and Physics. I was introduced to computer programming and robotics at my high school and have continued to have a passion for creation ever since.

Personal Statement

I swore as a small child to claim the power of the keyboard, and use it to make the world a cooler place with better tech.

I became interested in using my power to make awesome robots, and during my sojourn at UCF, discovered that robots are even cooler when they fly. I therefore endeavoured to create a sweet flying robot which would be able to take in information and avoid flying into people, since doing that would violate Isaac Asimov's first law of robotics.

I'm always interested in a good puzzle, which is probably why I was drawn to programming. There are endless solutions to endless problems, and even when you've found a solution which works, there's another puzzle in how to make it more efficient, or faster.
